Artist of the Month: Aurora Asknes

This month, we're beaming the spotlight on the ethereal and talented Aurora Asknes, better known simply as Aurora. Buckle up, because this artist is out-of-this-world (literally).

Image by @auroramusic.


Aurora Aksnes, known professionally as AURORA (all caps), is a Norwegian singer-songwriter and record producer. Born in Stavanger and raised in Oslo, Norway, she began writing songs and learning dance at a young age.

She gained recognition with her debut EP, Running with the Wolves (2015), which included the hit song "Runaway."

Let's be honest, AURORA and her song make her look like sort of a forest goddess or an angel of some sort lost in our world and trying to go back home (maybe that explains her song "Runaway"?). Her music and her voice is just ethereal and pure magic. And yes, zero autotune detected on those incredible high notes of hers.
