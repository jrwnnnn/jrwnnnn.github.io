CS1A Server Changelog

by pylix

February 14, 2025


Shattered Wilds:

  • Shattered Wilds is live! :pog.gif:
  • Added 90+ surface and cave biomes. :dance.gif:
  • Added 51 new structures. :pikaomg.gif:
  • Added 10 new enchantments
  1. Antidote - Negates all damage from poison and deals it to the chestplate. Incompatible with Protection enchantments.
  2. Curse of conductivity - Gives a chance to get struck by lightning during a thunderstorm.
  3. Illagers bane - Deal extra damage to Illagers, Vexes, Witches and Ravagers. Incompatible with Sharpness, Smite, Bane of Arthropods.
  4. Outreach - Raises the block interaction range.
  5. Photosynthesis - Repairs the item when is in direct view of the sun :pepestonks.webp:. Incompatible with Mending.
  6. Traveler - Faster sprinting speed and step up full blocks. Incompatible with Soul speed.
  7. Might - Power for crossbow. +0.5 damage added per level to arrows fired from this crossbow.
  8. Blowthrough - Piercing for bow. Allows an arrow to go through mobs.
  9. Tri-shot - Multishot for bows. Makes the bow fire 3 arrows.
  10. Spitefull - Below 2.5 hearts players are given +2 (+2 per level) attack damage. If hit by a mob there is a 30% (+15% per level) chance to get Strength 1 for 1-3 seconds.
  • Added the following gameplay mechanics for shattered wilds:
  1. Buffed monster's resistance, damage, and speed.
  2. Slightly increased spawn limits which means slightly more mobs will spawn.
  3. You got insomnia. Sleeping is disabled in the wilds.
  4. Disabled respawn points. Dying in the Shattered Wilds will always respawn you to the overworld.
  5. Disabled TPA commands.
  6. Disabled the nether.
Server-wide:
  • Disabled creeper griefing.
  • [Req #0001] As requested by someone :f-u.gif:, you can now instantly chop a tree by cutting one block. Axes lose durability depending on logs cut. Won't work when sneaking.
  • Geyser has been updated to support Bedrock 1.21.6.
