Artist of the Month: flipturn

This month, I'm beaming down the spotlight to the band that the time i discovered it became staple to my playlists, flipturn.

Image by @flipturnband.


Flipturn, stylized as flipturn, is an American indie rock band from Fernandina Beach, Florida formed in 2015. The band consists of founding members Dillon Basse (vocals), Tristan Duncan (guitar) and Madeline Jarman (bass) who founded the band as high school seniors.

Flipturn gained traction in 2018 with their EP Citrona. The band signed to Dualtone Records in June 2022. They released their debut album Shadowglow in August 2022, which received positive reception. The album release was followed by a tour through the United States in late 2022 into 2023 and the prestigious lineups of Lollapalooza, Governor’s Ball, Bonnaroo, Shaky Knees & SXSW.

In November 2023, the band announced its Something More Tour due to take place in Spring 2024, with support from fellow indie rock band Richy Mitch and the Coal Miners. Wikipedia↗

My New BFF

Flipturn’s music is like sunshine bottled up and poured into your ears. It’s not just happy—it’s the kind of “Life might be a shitshow, but we’re going to dance through it anyway :dance.gif:.” Their sound is pure, unfiltered summer, with just the right amount of nostalgia to make you forget all the crap you’re dealing with and just enjoy the ride :dodge.webp:.
